Strategy Overview

Technology has the power to transform education for every learner in Ghana. The EdTech Strategy brings together partners and policymakers to ensure that digital solutions support teaching, inclusion, and lifelong learning nationwide.

Governance, Management & Accountability

Enhancing leadership, coordination, and data-driven management through transparent systems to ensure effective governance and impact.

Infrastructure, Connectivity & Capacity Building

Improving digital infrastructure, connectivity, and teacher capacity for effective ICT integration in education.

Cybersecurity & Data Protection

Ensuring a safe and secure digital learning environment by protecting learner data, safeguarding systems, and promoting responsible technology use.

Innovation, Sustainability & Inclusion

Advancing inclusive, innovative, and sustainable digital learning systems for equitable education and lasting impact.
